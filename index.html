Welcome to Velan

Welcome to Velan — the heart and soul of natural, flavorful culinary experiences. Since our establishment in 2006 as a proud unit of Ashwin Foods, our mission has remained simple yet powerful: to bring authentic, natural, and wholesome food products to your kitchen. From the very beginning, Velan has stood for purity, taste, and trust — values that continue to guide us with every product we create. Over the years, our journey has been shaped by an unwavering passion for food. We believe that great taste begins with great ingredients, which is why we carefully select only the finest natural resources. Every product we craft reflects a deep respect for nature's goodness, traditional food wisdom, and modern hygiene standards. At Velan, we don't just create food — we create experiences. Delicious, memorable, and nourishing. Each bite is designed to delight your senses while ensuring wellness for you and your family. Whether it's a nostalgic homemade flavor or an innovative culinary twist, Velan products are made to bring comfort, joy, and authenticity to your everyday meals. With dedication, innovation, and love for good food, Velan continues to grow while remaining true to its purpose — to deliver nature-inspired goodness with uncompromising quality. Because at Velan, your health, taste, and trust always come first.
